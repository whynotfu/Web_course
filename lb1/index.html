ABOUT US

Food Construct — delivery of business lunches and hot meals across Moscow. We cook using only fresh ingredients and deliver your food exactly on time. No need to waste time cooking — just choose your dishes on the website and wait for the courier.

Each menu is created by our chef to ensure perfect balance between taste and nutrition. We offer meat, fish, and vegetarian meals, fresh salads, desserts, and beverages.

We work with both individual and corporate clients. Delivery is available to offices, events, and meetings — always hot, neatly packed, and ready to serve.
